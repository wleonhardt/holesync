# Voice: boring on purpose

**Decision (2026-07-03, William):** holesync's public voice — README, docs,
log messages, release notes — is *calm, dry, understated sysadmin confidence*.
"The boring way to keep your Pi-holes in sync." Safety and predictability are
the product; the writing should sound like it.

Rules of the voice:
- Boring is a feature. Frame reliability, guards, and diff-gating as the star.
- At most one wry line per section. Never memes, never exclamation points.
- Every claim stays technically precise; humor never replaces a fact.
- Honest about trade-offs (e.g. the Teleporter-tools comparison names what
  they do better).

Rejected alternatives:
- **Cosmic/Pi-hole-native wordplay** (gravity, FTL, orbits) — charming but
  ages badly and fights precision.
- **Pure spec-sheet minimalism** — accurate but gives no reason to trust or
  choose the tool.
